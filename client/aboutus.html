About us

 

Election Buzz provides insights into presidential candidates and their followers.

It helps you dig through candidate's tweets to figure out what they are talking about major subjects such as education, security, immigration, among others. You can also compare how much their followers are talking about the same subjects. Are they in line?

Using Election Buzz you can also find out who are candidate's top followers (be prepared for some surprises!) and learn how much each candidate has talked about certain topics over time. You can type any topic you want and check the graph. Did their priorities changed during the campaign?
