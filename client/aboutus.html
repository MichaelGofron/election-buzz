What is Election Buzz?

 

Election Buzz is a tool that provides insights into presidential candidates and their followers using information from Twitter.

We know there are a lot of people tweeting about the election and it can be overwhelming. So we dug through the candidates' tweets to figure out what they are talking about (education? security? how about immigration?). You can also compare how much their followers are talking about the same subjects. Do their tweets match up?

We also have other great features: Candidates' top followers (be prepared for some surprises!), a word cloud timeline and a graph of how much candidates are discussing topics of your choice.
